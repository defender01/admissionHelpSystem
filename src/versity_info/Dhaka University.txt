Dhaka University is the first established university in bangladesh region in the year 1921.It in known as Oxford of the East.
This university allocates 21 residential halls among them 16 for male students and 5 for female students.Every year more than
7 thousands students get admitted in this university.Threre are 1680 seats for A unit,2221 seats for B unit,1250 seats for C 
unit and 1416 seats for D unit.
